Cloud Operations and Management Portfolio

Module e-Portfolio (2025)

This portfolio documents my learning journey through twelve units in cloud computing, covering foundational concepts, hands-on skills, security, architecture, automation, and industry applications.

Introduction

Cloud computing has fundamentally transformed technology, shifting how organizations manage IT through on-demand infrastructure (IaaS), platform abstraction (PaaS), and software delivery (SaaS). With service models deployed across public, private, hybrid, and community clouds, the field is defined by automation, virtualization, containerization, and emerging AI/edge trends.

  • IaaS, PaaS, SaaS fundamentals
  • Deployment models: Public, Private, Hybrid
  • On-demand self-service, resource pooling, elasticity
Reflection:

Gained comprehensive understanding of cloud models and environments. Compared AWS, Google Cloud, Azure for differences in scalability and service portfolios. Recognized multi-cloud benefits and complexities for governance.
